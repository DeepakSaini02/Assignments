The Prime Minister shared photos of his meet with the Pope on Twitter and wrote: “Had a very warm meeting with Pope Francis. I had the opportunity to discuss a wide range of issues with him and also invited him to visit India”.
Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Saturday met Pope Francis in the Vatican City where they discussed “a wide range of issues”. It was the first-ever one-to-one meeting between PM Modi and Pope Francis wherein he invited the head of the Catholic Church to pay a visit to India.
